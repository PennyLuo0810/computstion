The blue bus

Someone gets on the bus at Stop Noisy,

and someone gets off at Stop Lonely.

Someone is at Stop Vow and gets off the bus.

Someone gets off at Stop Breaking-up.

Someone gets on the train at the Stop Struggle,

and someone gets off at Stop Ideal.

They separate at each life fragment at each platform, station and stop. On a snowy morning, Eunice got on a bus to find Stop Eternity. But where was Stop Eternity?
